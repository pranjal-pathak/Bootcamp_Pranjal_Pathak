When designing the Ancient Scripts Archive, we've chosen a custom charset that aligns with the specific ancient scripts we're presenting. In this case, we've set the charset to "ISO-8859-8," which is designed for the Hebrew script. This choice is essential for accurately rendering the unique characters and symbols found in ancient Hebrew texts. By using this custom charset, we ensure that the website's content is displayed in a way that reflects the historical and cultural significance of the scripts, making it easier for our visitors to engage with and appreciate the beauty of these ancient writing systems.
