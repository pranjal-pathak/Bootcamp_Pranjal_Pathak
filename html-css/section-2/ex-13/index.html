Letterboxd Frequently Asked Questions

What is Letterboxd?

Letterboxd is a social platform for film enthusiasts. It allows users to create lists of movies they've watched, write and read reviews, follow other cinephiles, and discover new films. It's a place to share your love for cinema and engage with a community of movie lovers.

How do I create a Letterboxd account?

Creating a Letterboxd account is easy. Visit the Letterboxd website and click on the "Sign Up" or "Join Letterboxd" button. You can sign up using your email or link your account to a social media profile. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up your account and start exploring the world of film.

Is Letterboxd free to use?

Yes, Letterboxd offers both free and paid memberships. While the free membership provides access to many features, including the ability to log and review films, the paid Pro and Patron memberships offer additional perks like advanced statistics, ad-free browsing, and more. You can choose the membership that suits your needs.

How can I connect with other users on Letterboxd?

You can connect with other users by following their profiles. When you follow someone, you'll see their activity on your dashboard, and you can interact by liking, commenting, and sharing their reviews and lists. Letterboxd is a great place to discover like-minded movie enthusiasts and engage in discussions about films.

Can I use Letterboxd on mobile devices?

Yes, Letterboxd offers mobile apps for both iOS and Android devices. You can download the app from your device's app store and access Letterboxd on the go. The app provides a user-friendly interface for logging movies, reading reviews, and staying connected with the community.
